What is Semantic Markup?

According to HTML and CSS- Design and build Website 2011 by John Duckett there are some text elements that are not intended to affect the structure of your web pages, but they do add extra information to the pages â€” they are known as semantic markup.

Why is Semantic Markup Important?

Firstly, when a search engine comes across your webpage it doesn't read decorative tags like <b>, <i> neither does it pay attention to the style of the webpage. The only way to undestand which elements are more important is through marking.
